**Quarter Acre Wonderland** is a gardening blog celebrating the magic of small-space gardening on Colorado's Western Slope.

I'm Leesa, and I garden on a quarter acre in the Grand Valley. This blog is where I share seasonal planting guides, garden design ideas, tool reviews, and everything I'm learning about growing food and beauty in our unique high-desert climate.

The Western Slope presents its own set of challenges — alkaline soil, intense sun, late frosts, and dry summers — but it's also a remarkable place to garden. With the right approach, a quarter acre is more than enough to grow something extraordinary.

Whether you're a seasoned grower or just getting your hands dirty for the first time, I hope you'll find something useful here. Come grow with us.
